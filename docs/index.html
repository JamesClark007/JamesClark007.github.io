About the Buisness

The COFFEE CONNECTION was established by the WESTSIDE VINEYARD in the year 2000 on the location of the former Bruno's Ristorante, a landmark Italian restaurant on the Los Angeles Westside. The formation of culture around coffee and coffeehouses dates to 14th century. Over the centuries, coffeehouses in Western Europe and the Eastern Mediterranean were traditionally social hubs, as well as artistic and intellectual centers. In keeping with these traditions, the COFFEE CONNECTION provides a number of exquisite spaces ideal for socialization, study, collegial gatherings and performing arts on stage both large and small. Our wifi is fast and free, and we provide our own dedicated, secure and unlimited wifi for the EXCLUSIVE use of Coffee Connection patrons.
